Games

YINA is a third-person platformer available on Steam. The science fiction game is largely story focused, though the player can also enjoy No-Man's-Sky-style cataloguing, as well as collecting and grinding for currency.
Spinny Adventures is a third-person fantasy game available on Steam. You play as Spindelin "Spinny" Merryberry, a young farmer/handyperson/entreprenure/magician living in the coastal town of Birchvale.
CERE:S is an educational first-person science fiction game available on Steam. Subjects including mathematics, physics, biology and computer science are available to learn and practice by children, teens, and adults.
